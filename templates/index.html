About the institution

Bon Secours College for Women is a gift of God in the higher education ministry undertaken by the Franciscan Sisters of Our Lady of Bon Secours, a Religious congregation which is committed to the welfare of women for decades. The college started its long journey of reaching out to the poor and rural young women in the year 2002.
The College is affiliated to Bharathidasan University, Tiruchirappalli and recognized by UGC under 2(f) and 12 (B) in 2009 and 2011 respectively. The college is accredited by NAAC with A++ grade (CGPA 3.71) in the second cycle on 13.12.2021. Bon Secours College is one of the renowned institutions which is ranked by NIRF in the 151-200 Band.
The College is located in an area of 16 Acres with 60% greenery and with a built up area of 24993.57 Sq. Mts. The location of college in a sylvan surrounding provides perfect atmosphere for learning. The campus is calm, peaceful and serene. The plastic free, eco-friendly and clean and green surrounding provides a proper background for the pursuit of knowledge
Bon Secours College delivers the most professional and personalized education and training to the students to equip them academically strong with professional ingenuity and social commitment. The college focuses on quality culture of the institution in terms of IQAC towards Quality Initiatives, Sustenance and Enhancement as reflected in Vision and Mission. Aligning with NAAC's conviction various schemes and programme are introduced to fulfil the seven criteria.

Our Institution offers a wide range of programme options and courses in tune with the emerging national trends and skill-development programmes pertaining to value added, vocational certificate courses and special programme through GEM (Gear up Extraordinary Minds).
At Bon Secours, the quality of teaching and learning depends largely on teacher readiness to draw upon recent available technological support and initiative to develop learning resources, familiarity with learning management systems, e-resources to enrich teaching-learning through TIID (Teacher Individual and Institutional Development) programme.
The research culture among students and faculties is realized through KATT (Knowledge and Technology Transfer) programme. Specially designed Inputs are provided to the needy students with learning difficulties and finance through SALP (Slow and Advanced Learner Programme) and SSS(Student Support Services) programme respectively.
The college also extends opportunities towards social responsibilities and distinctiveness through part-V activities and under GBS (Give Back to Society) programme. It also intends to serve the society through the associations and Student Council. Our institution serves as a participating institution under Unnat Bharat Abiyan (UBA) 2.0 and has adopted 5 villages, and offers transformational change in rural development. On the whole Bon Secours extends its services towards the holistic development of the student community to transform them as Nation builders.

About the department


The Department of Management Studies was established in 2004 with the motto of Commitment, Quality, and Excellence. It aims to transform women learners from rural backgrounds into capable administrators on the global stage. Through undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs, the department strives to equip students with the skills and competencies required for success in business and management. Focusing on practical abilities like problem-solving, communication, leadership, teamwork, and project management, the department not only imparts management theory but also nurtures critical thinking and analytical skills. By offering supplementary courses, it elevates knowledge and skills, fostering holistic development. Experiential learning is a cornerstone, allowing students to engage with the real world through internships, fieldwork, research, industrial visits, and idea-generation platforms. This approach bridges the gap between theory and practice, enhancing graduates' readiness for their professional journeys. The department's success lies in its teaching and learning practices. Students shine by securing University ranks and excelling in state and national Management meets, a testament to the quality of education provided. The achievements of alumni in their careers underscore the department's effectiveness in preparing students for the competitive world. The Department of Management Studies is committed to intellectual excellence. It offers a platform for exposure to modern ideas and intellectual transformations, dedicated to student growth and betterment. As it grooms students for success, the department's journey aligns with its initial vision and continues to shape capable leaders for the future.
