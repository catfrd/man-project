Master of Masters (Business Plan)

  1. Each group would be limited to a maximum of six members.
  2. The performance will last 5 minutes.
  3. During the performance, background music (without lyrics) provided by the team will be played.
  4. Participants must have a pen drive with them to play background music throughout their performance.
  5. There are no props permitted.
  6. There should be no obscene content in play.
  7. The participant should perform based on the themes 'Ethical Issues in Business' and 'Corporate Social Responsibilities.'
  8. The decision of the jury will be final.
